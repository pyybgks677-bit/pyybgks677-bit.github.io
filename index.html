About this mock test

This mock test is designed to help you practise multiple-choice questions and to get used to the pace of a timed test. It includes two modes:

  • Practice mode – review results and retry incorrect questions.
  • Exam mode – results at the end, to simulate an exam-style attempt.

This is a practice tool only. It is not an official exam and passing this mock test does not guarantee success in any official test.

How to use

  • Choose your mode on the quiz page (Practice or Exam).
  • Optionally shuffle the question order.
  • Answer every question before submitting (submit is disabled until all questions are answered).
  • You can move freely and change answers before submitting.

Keyboard shortcuts on the quiz page: A/B/C/D to answer, Left/Right arrows to move, F to flag, S to skip.
